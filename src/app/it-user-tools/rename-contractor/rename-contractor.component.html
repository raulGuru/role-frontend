Rename an Associate or Contractor Enterprise LDAP ID
Certain business applications are severely impacted by ID renames. If a particular rename would affect one of these applications and the force checkbox is left unchecked the rename will fail and an email will be sent to you and the affected support team(s) to prepare for the intended ID rename. You should receive approval email(s) from the impacted areas within 24 hours. Once approval is received the rename can be retried with the force option checked.
